This is CarWash service ver 1.0

Used LinkedHashMap and ArrayList to make Menu and Services List.
Use method Get and Equal to navigate within menu.
It is necessary to input full name of the service or vehicle type to choose corresponding option.
bill or Bill - shows current bill with already chosen position.
back or Back - returns to menu with vehicle input.
print or Print - shows "Basket" list of chosen services and "Bill" - list and quantity of chosen services.
q - finish program and prints all ordered services and sum to pay.
